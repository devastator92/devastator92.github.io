The following tables list the major features of every SSH implementation we have encountered, sorted by platform, excluding the Unix products discussed in the previous part of the book (SSH1, SSH2, OpenSSH, F-Secure SSH).  Meaning

 The product name. If followed by "(recommended)," we have evaluated the program and recommend its use. If a product isn't listed as recommended, it might still be good, but we didn't evaluate it thoroughly.
