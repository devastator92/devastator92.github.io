4.6. Summary

SSH1, SSH2, F-Secure SSH Server, and OpenSSH may all be tailored in various ways by compile-time configuration with the configure script. We've covered the SSH-specific flags, but remember that other operating system-specific flags may also apply to your installation, so be sure to read the installation notes supplied with the software.

Once installed, SSH software may replace the insecure r-commands on your Unix system, not only when run directly, but also within other programs that invoke rsh, such as Emacs and Pine.
