F.3. Final Words on SNMPv3

While vendors have begun to support SNMPv3 in their products, keep in mind that it is still a draft standard, not a full standard. If you would like to keep track of SNMPv3 happenings, you can visit the Internet Engineering Task Force's (IETF) SNMPv3 working group site at http://www.ietf.org/html.charters/snmpv3-charter.html.
