13.6. Getting Help

The MRTG web site, http://www.mrtg.org, offers a great deal of information and help. You can subscribe to the MRTG mailing list from this page. MRTG is also discussed frequently in the Usenet newsgroup comp.dcom.net-management. Finally, don't ignore MRTG's documentation, which is located in the doc subdirectory of the MRTG distribution. The documentation is included in both text and HTML form and is fairly complete and comprehensive.
